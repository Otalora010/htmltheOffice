The Office: Dunder Mifflin

The Office is a comedy series that follows the daily lives of employees at Dunder Mifflin.

Michael Scott

Michael Scott is the regional manager of Dunder Mifflin. He is known for his inappropriate humor and childish behavior, but deep down he truly cares about his employees and wants to be loved and respected by everyone in the office.

Jim Halpert

Jim is a salesman with a relaxed attitude and a great sense of humor. He is famous for his clever pranks on Dwight and his sarcastic personality, but he is also hardworking and deeply loyal to the people he cares about.

Pam Beesly

Pam starts as the receptionist but later follows her dreams of becoming an artist and a salesperson. She is kind, patient, and emotionally intelligent, often acting as the heart of the office and supporting her coworkers.

Dwight Schrute

Dwight is a dedicated and intense employee who takes his job very seriously. As Assistant to the Regional Manager, he is extremely loyal to Michael and follows rules strictly, often leading to funny and exaggerated situations.

Holly Flax

Holly is the HR representative who shares a very similar personality to Michael. She is warm, kind, and understanding, and her relationship with Michael shows a more genuine and emotional side of both characters.

Stanley Hudson

Loves pretzels and hates meetings. Always serious.

Kelly Kapoor

Loves drama, gossip, and attention.

Ryan Howard

The temp who becomes ambitious and complicated.
